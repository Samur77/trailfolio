Automated Account Update Process

Designed and implemented a scalable flow to streamline bulk updates of account records, eliminating the need for manual data entry. The process validates critical fields, such as "Type" and "Rating", which are key to shipment routing and customer prioritization, while ensuring that incomplete data is not unintentionally overwritten. This automation enhances operational efficiency, ensures data integrity, and provides logistics coordinators and account managers with accurate, up-to-date client information, which is essential for planning, communication, and service optimization.
